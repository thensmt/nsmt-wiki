# NSMT Brand

**Designed by:** Maegan Makes
**Google Drive:** [Logo Assets](https://drive.google.com/open?id=132MxzH0nYgXy7VBCXunER6Aw6NbSTrIi)

## Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| Black | `#000000` | 0, 0, 0 | Backgrounds, borders |
| Blue | `#0E80FC` | 14, 128, 252 | Primary accent |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Text on dark backgrounds |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Short headers / titles ONLY | "Design" font (web fallback: Bebas Neue) | Use sparingly — headlines and titles only |
| Subheaders and body text | Rubik | Primary reading font across all contexts |

## Logo Types

### Primary
Blue "NSMT" wordmark above a black bar containing white "NOVA SPORTS MEDIA TEAM" text.

### Secondary / Boxed
"NSMT" in blue inside a rounded rectangle with a black border. Compact lockup for constrained spaces.

### Wordmark
Just the text "NSMT" in bold italic. Available in three color variants: blue, black, and white.

## Usage Rules

- Blue (`#0E80FC`) is always the primary accent color
- Black for backgrounds and borders
- White for text on dark backgrounds
- Use SVG for web, PNG for raster contexts
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logos outside the defined variants
