# Camera Operator

## Top Camera Operator

**Required for minimum crew:** Yes
**Equipment:** [Sony FX3](../equipment/cameras.md) + lens on [SmallRig AD-100 FreeBlazer tripod](../equipment/cameras.md), [intercom headset](../equipment/comms.md)
**Can double as:** Nothing — hands are on the camera

### Responsibilities
- Operates hero/wide shot from elevated position
- Follows the ball and primary action
- Provides the default "safe" shot the TD can always cut to

### Setup Checklist
1. Mount FX3 on SmallRig AD-100 tripod at elevated position
2. Attach [Teradek Ace 750 TX](../equipment/video-transport.md) to camera rig
3. Power on V-mount battery (see [Power](../equipment/power.md))
4. Confirm wireless link with Teradek RX at production table
5. Frame court for full-court wide shot
6. Put on [Hollyland intercom](../equipment/comms.md) headset, confirm comms with TD

## Baseline Camera Operator(s)

**Required for minimum crew:** 1 (2 ideal — home and away baselines)
**Equipment:** [Camera](../equipment/cameras.md) + [SmallRig Classic 4480 shoulder rig](../equipment/cameras.md) + rolling chair, [intercom headset](../equipment/comms.md)
**Can double as:** Nothing during live play

### Responsibilities
- Positioned at baseline (home side and/or away side)
- Tracks action as it comes toward the basket
- Gets player reactions, close-ups, bench shots
- Follows TD direction via [intercom](../equipment/comms.md)

### Setup Checklist
1. Assemble shoulder rig with camera
2. Attach [Teradek Ace 750 TX](../equipment/video-transport.md)
3. Power on V-mount battery (see [Power](../equipment/power.md))
4. Confirm wireless link with Teradek RX
5. Position rolling chair at baseline
6. Put on intercom headset, confirm comms with TD

## During Broadcast
- Listen for TD calls on intercom ("go tight on #23", "get the bench", "ready for your shot")
- Keep a steady shot — the shoulder rig + rolling chair combo allows smooth lateral tracking
- During dead balls, get crowd shots, coach reactions, scoreboard

## Common Issues
- Teradek signal loss: stay within range, check battery on TX
- Shoulder rig fatigue: take breaks during timeouts, switch shoulders
- Rolling chair drift: ensure floor surface allows smooth rolling, avoid cables
