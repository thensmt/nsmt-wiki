# Power

## V-Mount Battery System

**Standard:** V-mount batteries across all rigs
**Used by:** All camera and video transport equipment

### What V-Mount Powers

| Equipment | Adapter Needed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Teradek Ace 750 TX (per camera) | Direct V-mount plate | On camera rig |
| Teradek Ace 750 RX (at table) | Direct V-mount plate | At production table |
| Camcorders (PXW-Z200 / NX800) | V-mount to BP-U30 adapter plate | See fitment note below |
| Sony FX3 | V-mount to NP-FZ100 adapter | On top camera rig |

### BP-U30 Adapter Plates (Camcorder Route)

If going with camcorders ([cameras decision pending](cameras.md)), V-mount to BP-U30 adapter plates are needed.

- **Brands:** KOCACKOO or Alvins
- **Dummy battery box:** 38mm tall
- **Open item:** Verify the dummy battery box physically fits the camcorder battery compartment before purchasing

### Spare NP-FZ100 Batteries

Keep spare NP-FZ100 batteries for the Sony FX3 cameras. These are the native FX3 battery and serve as backup if V-mount adapter fails or for quick swaps.

## Hollyland Solidcom SE Batteries

The [Hollyland Solidcom SE intercom](comms.md) headsets use built-in rechargeable batteries.

**CRITICAL:** Batteries died during Hoopfest broadcast on 2026-03-22. **Spare batteries or a charging solution must be in place for every future event.** See [Hoopfest event report](../events/hoopfest-2026-03-22.md).
