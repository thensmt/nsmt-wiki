# ADR-003: Baseline Cameras

**Date:** 2026-03
**Status:** proposed

## Context

NSMT broadcasts need 2 baseline cameras for court-length coverage. The current inventory includes 2x Sony FX3, but their 24-70mm zoom range is limiting for baseline positions that need to cover the full length of the court.

## Options Considered

| Option | Camera | Price (each) | Pros | Cons |
|--------|--------|-------------|------|------|
| A (recommended) | Sony PXW-Z200 | ~$4,000 | 20x zoom, SDI + HDMI output | Higher cost |
| B | Sony HXR-NX800 | ~$3,300 | Good zoom range | HDMI only (no SDI) |
| C | Keep all FX3 | — | Already owned | Need additional lenses, limited zoom range |

## Decision

**Pending.** Option A (Sony PXW-Z200) is the recommended path.

## Consequences

- Dedicated camcorders are pick-up-and-shoot for baseline operators, reducing setup complexity
- FX3 cameras are freed up for the cinematic hero shot from the top/center position
- If going with camcorders, V-mount to BP-U30 adapters will be needed for battery compatibility
